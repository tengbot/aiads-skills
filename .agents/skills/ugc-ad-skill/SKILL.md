# UGC Ad Skill

## Purpose

Use this Skill when an AI agent needs to create UGC-style ad scripts or video briefs.

Built for [AIADS.IM](https://aiads.im), an AI Ads Generator for UGC video ads and product video ads.

## Inputs

Ask for or infer:

- product name
- product category
- target customer
- key pain point
- desired outcome
- proof point
- offer
- creator persona
- platform
- duration
- tone

## UGC angles

Generate at least 6 angles:

1. I tried this so you do not have to
2. Problem to solution
3. Honest review
4. Before and after
5. Three reasons why
6. Mistake correction
7. Routine integration
8. Unboxing and first impression
9. Comparison with old method
10. Founder or expert POV

## Output format

Return:

- 6 ad angles
- 10 hooks
- 3 short scripts
- scene-by-scene storyboard
- caption lines
- CTA variants
- notes for video generation

## Rules

- Do not invent real customer testimonials.
- Do not claim guaranteed results.
- Keep the tone platform-native.
- Write like a creator, not a corporate ad.
- Keep scenes simple enough for AI video generation.
