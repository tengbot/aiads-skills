# Ad Script Template

Use this template to turn a product brief into a short ad script.

Main site: https://aiads.im

## Script brief

```yaml
product:
category:
audience:
pain_point:
benefit:
proof_point:
offer:
platform:
duration:
tone:
creator_persona:
objection:
```

## 15 second structure

```text
0-3s: Hook
3-6s: Problem or desire
6-10s: Product reveal and benefit
10-13s: Proof, demo, or reason to believe
13-15s: CTA
```

## 30 second structure

```text
0-3s: Hook
3-7s: Problem or desire
7-12s: Product reveal
12-20s: Demo, benefit, or transformation
20-25s: Objection handling or proof
25-30s: CTA
```

## Output format

```yaml
angle:
hook:
script_15s:
  - timestamp:
    visual:
    voiceover:
    on_screen_text:
script_30s:
  - timestamp:
    visual:
    voiceover:
    on_screen_text:
cta_options:
caption_options:
video_generation_notes:
```

## Rules

- Do not invent proof.
- Do not invent reviews.
- Do not promise guaranteed outcomes.
- Keep the language specific and visual.
- Make the first shot obvious.
